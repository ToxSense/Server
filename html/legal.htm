ToxSense

This project is imagined and created by Timo Bilhöfer, Markus Pfaff and Maria Radulescu.

The map engine is provided by leaflet, map tiles are from OpenStreetMaps and the non-AI sensor values are syncronised from the sensor.community.
The AI-Data is generated by the ToxSense-Headband or the ToxSense-Interpolation-AI.

As part of the seminar Learning Atmospheres in the winter-semester 2020/21 it is supported by Irina Auernhammer, Silas Kalmbach and Prof. Lucio Blandini from the Institute for Lightweight Structures and Conceptual Design (ILEK) and is also part of the Collaborative Research Centre 1244 (SFB 1244).
